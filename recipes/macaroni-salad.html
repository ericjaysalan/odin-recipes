Macaroni Salad

Home

Ingredients

  • Elbow/macaroni pasta
  • All purpose cream
  • Raisins
  • Condensed milk
  • Fruit cocktail
  • Mayonnaise
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Put the all purpose cream at the freezer. Let it chill.
  2. Boil water. Add salt and oil.
  3. Cook pasta in boiling water until al dente.
  4. Strain pasta and let it cool.
  5. Once the pasta cools down, add the chilled all purpose cream, condensed milk, mayonnaise, and fruit cocktail to the pasta and mix.
  6. Cut the cheese into cubes and add to the pasta.
  7. Add the raisins
  8. Put in the refrigirator for 5 hours.
