Filipino-style Spaghetti

Home

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti sauce
  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Onion
  • Corned beef
  • Hotdog
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil water. Add oil and salt.
  2. Add pasta to the boiling water. Cook until al dente.
  3. Strain pasta. Let cool.
  4. Saute onions at medium high heat.
  5. Add pasta sauce once the onion browns. Reduce heat once it bubbles and let simmer.
  6. Add corned beef and hotdogs.
  7. Add the sauce to the pasta. Mix evenly.
  8. Add cheese and mix.
