Mango Float Recipe

Ingredients

  • Mangoes
  • All purpose cream
  • Condensed milk
  • Graham crackers

Steps

  1. Whip all purpose cream and then add condensed milk to make the filling.
  2. Put a layer of graham at the bottom of a container.
  3. Add the filling.
  4. Add the mangoes
  5. Repeat step 3 & 4 until the container is full. The last layer should contain filling.
  6. Break some graham crackers and add as garnish to the last layer.
  7. Let chill for at least 5 hours.
